## Architecture Notes

- Banking transactions are processed in real-time through the fraud detection engine
- Insurance claims undergo automated risk scoring before approval
- Stock trades are monitored by the market surveillance system
- All flagged events are routed to the investigation workflow
- Final reports are generated and stored in the analysis/ directory
